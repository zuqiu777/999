Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Introduction

FlashScore Helper ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

FlashScore Helper collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • User Preferences: Theme settings (dark/light mode), notification preferences, and auto-refresh settings.
  • Favorite Teams: A list of match IDs that you mark as favorites.

All data is stored using Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local) and never leaves your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, etc.).
  • We do not track your browsing history.
  • We do not use cookies or third-party tracking.
  • We do not send any data to external servers.
  • We do not collect analytics or usage statistics.

Permissions

The Extension uses the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage: To save your preferences (theme, favorites) locally on your device.
  • alarms: To enable periodic badge updates when auto-refresh is enabled.

Third-Party Services

When you click "Open FlashScore," the Extension opens FlashScore.co.th in a new browser tab. Your interaction with FlashScore is governed by FlashScore's own privacy policy. The Extension does not inject any code or collect data from FlashScore.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage, your data is protected by your operating system's security. We do not transmit, process, or store any data remotely.

Children's Privacy

This Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The Extension provides sports score information suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page for FlashScore Helper.
